# SaaS Business Performance Dashboard (Tableau)

## 🎯 Goal
To provide a 360-degree interactive view of SaaS business health, enabling executives to track growth, retention, and financial stability in real-time.

## 📊 Key Metrics Analyzed
* **Revenue Metrics:** MRR (Monthly Recurring Revenue), ARR (Annual Recurring Revenue), and ARPU.
* **Customer Success:** Net Revenue Retention (NRR), Churn Rate, and Expansion Revenue.
* **Profitability:** Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC) and Lifetime Value (LTV).
